# City Pilot Program

**Public page:** `/pilot`  
**Priority:** P1 — execute before national marketing spend

---

## Goal

Validate product–market fit in **one city** with **5–10 real providers** (mix of solo barbers and shops) before scaling acquisition nationally.

---

## Cohort design

| Parameter | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Geography | Single metro (e.g. Athens, Thessaloniki — pick one) |
| Size | 5–10 active providers |
| Mix | ≥1 multi-chair shop + ≥2 solo barbers |
| Duration | 90 days active booking |
| Start | When 5 providers are live with direct links |

---

## Selection criteria

1. **Operational readiness** — willing to put booking link on Instagram/Google within 7 days  
2. **Feedback cadence** — 30-min call every 2 weeks  
3. **Data sharing** — anonymized no-show and booking counts for ROI case study  
4. **Champion** — at least one shop owner who can refer 2+ barbers  
5. **Not a fit** — cash-only, no smartphones, refuses any card-on-file policy  

---

## Provider offer (pilot perks)

- **Founding city** badge on Explore  
- **Locked pricing** for 12 months at pilot rates  
- White-glove onboarding (Setup Guide + optional founder call)  
- Priority support email / chat  
- Input on discovery-fee design before public launch  

---

## Weekly ops checklist (internal)

| Week | Focus |
|------|--------|
| 0 | Recruit 10 prospects → confirm 5–10 signed LOI / email yes |
| 1 | All profiles live, services + hours, Stripe Connect started |
| 2 | Direct links published; first 20 bookings tracked |
| 3 | No-show policy enabled where applicable; ROI interviews |
| 4–12 | Bi-weekly retro; fix top 3 friction items each sprint |
| 13 | Publish city case study; decide expand vs pivot |

---

## Success metrics

| Metric | Target (90 days) |
|--------|------------------|
| Providers live | ≥5 |
| Direct bookings / provider / month | ≥15 median |
| No-show rate vs baseline | ↓ 20% (self-reported or measured) |
| NPS (pilot cohort) | ≥40 |
| At least one ROI quote | “One no-show saved paid for the month” |

---

## Exit criteria → scale

Proceed to second city or paid marketing when:

- [ ] 5+ providers retained (still active at day 90)  
- [ ] Median provider completes ≥10 bookings/month  
- [ ] Documented ROI story from ≥2 providers  
- [ ] Support load sustainable (<2h/day founder time)
